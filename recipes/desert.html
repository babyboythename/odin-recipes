Desert

A delicious desert that you'll never get tired of eating. You haven't tasted anything better.

Ingredients:

  • 150ml milk
  • 70g oat flakes
  • 1 banana
  • 1 egg
  • 20g cocoa
  • 1/2 spoon bakimg powder
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar
  • vegatable oil
  • 80g Chocolate

Steps

  1. Put oats and milk in a bowl(bowl 1) and mix well
  2. Cut banana into pieces and crush them in a seperate bowl(bowl 2)
  3. add egg in bowl 2 and whisk together
  4. Add cocoa, baking powder & sugar in bowl 2 and whisk well
  5. Pour bowl 1 contents into bowl 2 and mix well
  6. Grease the inside of a baking bowl with vegetable oil and pour all bowl 2 contents into it
  7. Bake at 180 degrees for 30-35 minutes
  8. Melt chocolate in bowl 3 and add 1/2 cup of milk and mix well
  9. When done baking, pour bowl 3 into baked goods and let it sit until chocolate turns hard
