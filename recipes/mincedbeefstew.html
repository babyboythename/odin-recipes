Minced Beef Stew

This recipe is sure to make your taste buds water.

Ingredients:

  • An onion
  • Oil
  • 1 Garlic Clover
  • Mince Meat
  • Rice
  • 3 Tomatoes,1 Red,Green & Yellow Bell pepper
  • Salt
  • Potatoes
  • 1 Large tomamtoe & tomatoe paste

Steps

  1. Put 1 tbsp of oil in pan
  2. Grind beef and put in a seperate bowl
  3. Cut Onion into small pieces and cook till soft on medium heat
  4. Add grinded meat into pan, season with salt stir frequently
  5. Cut potatoe into small blocks and add to pan when meat changes colour
  6. Add chopped tomatoe and tomatoe paste to pan, mix well with a cup of water
  7. Chop bell peppers into blocks and add to pan
  8. Add any spices that you may like and let it simmer on medium heat for 5 min
  9. Cook Rice
